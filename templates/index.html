Am I Cooked?

A Job Visualisation For ICT Students

Overview

The ICT job market is a dynamic and fast-paced industry that offers many opportunities for professionals with the right skills and experience. In this report, we will provide an overview of the ICT job market in Singapore and the United States, including the most sought after jobs and skills in demand. We will also present data visualizations to help you better understand the job market and make informed decisions about your career.

Job Market Overview

Singapore

Singapore is a global hub for technology and innovation. The country has a strong ICT sector and is home to many multinational companies. The demand for ICT professionals in Singapore is high, with many job opportunities available in areas such as software development, cybersecurity, and data analytics.

US

The United States is a global leader in technology and innovation. The country has a large ICT sector and is home to many of the world's leading technology companies. The demand for ICT professionals in the US is high, with many job opportunities available in areas such as software development, cybersecurity, and data analytics.

Using Natural Language Processing(NLP), key skills were extracted from the job description dataset from both regions. The above graphs show the top skills in demand in Singapore and the US.

  • - Programming languages (eg. Java, Python, SQL) remain a strong demand from employers for both regions. This indicates that having a strong foundation in programming languages is essential for ICT professionals.
  • - In Singapore, there is a higher demand for skills related to cybersecurity and data protection (eg. systems, testing, compliance).
  • - In the US, there is a higher demand for managerial skills (eg. project management). Which is consistent with our observation that generalist are more favored in the US.
